Who We Are

Max has been a staple in the Bozeman area for years, and as soon as you step foot into his barbershop you'll understand why. We have stylists specializing in everything from men's hair, to perms, to eyebrows, whatever style needs you have, Max has you covered.

For almost 40 years Max has been styling and cutting hair in the Bozeman area. Making his deput appearance in 1984 with Bozeman Beauty College located on Montana State University's campus, Max spent years learning the Bozeman culture and how to best serve this community's needs. In 1990 he took over Mr. Mack's Bauty College and renamed it Max's Bozeman Beauty College.

Ten years later, Max took an oppurtunity to co-operate with his father at a new barbershop, Mack's and Max's Barbershop. In 2019 Max's Barber and Hair Affair opened for business, with Max now proudly serving the Bozeman area as the owner and operator
